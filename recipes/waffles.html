Waffles

Description

Fluffy waffles to kickstart your weekend mornings. Also a great addition to this easy fried chicken recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of your favorite pancake mix

  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

  • 1 egg

  • 3/4 cup milk

  • This list makes approximately two waffles, add more as necessary

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron to medium heat

  2. Mix all ingredients into bowl until fully combined

  3. Gently pour mix into waffle iron

  4. Remove waffles when your particular iron demands it

  5. Enjoy!
